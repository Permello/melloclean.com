Customer Communication & Support

- There's no way for clients to contact support from the dashboard (chat, message, or ticket). If a cleaner misses a spot
  or there's an issue, where does the client go?
- No notification preferences — clients should control how they get reminders (email, SMS, push) for upcoming appointments
  and payment due dates

Post-Service Experience

- No ratings/reviews — after a completed appointment, the client should be able to rate the cleaning and leave feedback.
  This is essential for quality control and retention
- No service history with details — beyond just "completed" status, clients want to see what was actually done (checklist
  of tasks completed)

Booking Gaps

- No service add-ons — when booking, clients often want extras (inside oven, inside fridge, laundry, window cleaning). The
  appointment form only captures service type, but not customizations
- No cancellation policy enforcement — if your policy requires 24-48 hour notice, the cancel flow should warn about late
  cancellation fees
- No re-book from past appointment — a "book again" button on completed appointments is one of the highest-converting
  features for cleaning companies
- No estimate/quote step — after filling the booking form, clients usually want to see a price estimate before confirming.
  Right now booking goes straight to creation

Client Preferences

- No special instructions field that persists across bookings — things like alarm codes, spare key location, pet info,
  parking instructions. These are critical for the cleaning crew
- Should clients be able to manage multiple service addresses? Some have a primary home + rental property + vacation home

Business Growth

- No referral program — a "refer a friend" link or code is a standard growth tool for cleaning companies. Even a simple
  share link with a discount
- No promotional offers / active discounts display

Trust & Safety

- No assigned cleaner visibility — clients want to know who's coming to their home. Showing cleaner name and maybe a photo
  builds trust
- No terms of service acceptance on first booking

Dashboard UX

- The overview page could prominently show the next upcoming appointment as a hero card, not just a count
- A "total spent" / "cleanings completed" lifetime stat helps clients feel the value of the relationship
